IEEE SB GVPCE(A)

Technology Awareness Program for School Children

Date: 26 -29 Feb 2018

Time: 03:00 PM - 05:00 PM

Event Category: Social Activity

Event Summary

The event was held on 26th - 29th February 2018 at ZPH School. The SB members have initiated awareness about technology program to the school children in nearby government and private schools. They interacted with the 8th-10th class students to tell them the future prospects about the education reforms and the technology development. They guided the students towards aiming their goals and how to reach them.
