IEEE SB GVPCE(A)

Demonstration of Electronic Circuits to School Children

Date: 17 Sep 2018

Time: 11:00 AM to 03:00 PM

Event Category: Social Activity

Event Summary

The event was held on 17th September 2018 at ZPH School, Chandrampalem , Madhurawada . It was organised by IEEE SB WIE GVPCE (A) supported by IEEE Vizag bay subsection. It was a technical demonstration given by Ch.Devi, B.Chinabala Revati of 4th year ECE-1 and V.Haritha, V.Sheetal of 2ndyear of ECE-4 coordinated by Dr. V.Leela Rani, Student Advisor IEEE WIE GVPCE(A).

The event has begun at 11:00 AM. The schedule time was 4 hrs.from 11:00A.M. to 3:00 P.M. The goal of the workshop is to motivate the school children towards technical knowledge enhancement. Live demonstration and presentation was given for electronic circuits like Mobile charger, automatic on-off lights and touch me not. Students are also motivated for doing good projects which will be useful for them in domestic activities.

B.Tech students are motivated toparticipate in these technical events to improve their domain knowledge. This will be useful for their preparation towards off and on campus job recruitments. Hence more number of events will be organized by the students in future to build up their career under IEEE Vizag Bay Sub Section.

The event started with the introduction speech by M.Rajababu , Head Master,ZPH School, Chandrampalem, Madhurawada introducing the student coordinators to the audience. There is a positive response from all the students and teachers. A photo session was followed by the above events. The Technical lecture and demonstration ended at 3:00PM and has also witnessed the presence of Dr. V.LeelaRani, Student Advisor, IEEE SB WIE GVPCE (A).
