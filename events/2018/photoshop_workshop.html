Event Summary

The worshop started with the IEEE SB counselor Dr. Satish Kumar Gudey addressing the student gathering. The event was held on 24th DECEMBER 2018 at Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering(A) Madhurwada. It was organized by IEEE SB GVPCE(A) supported by IEEE vizag bay sub section .The event has begun at 9:15AM. The gathering was addressed by Dr. G.V.E.Satish Kumar (student body counsellor), Dr. C.V.K.Bhanu P.G.Dean and Dr .Ravi Sarepalli.

There were a total of 63 students who participated in the workshop in which 12 members are IEEE members and 51 are non-IEEE members.

The workshop started with a small presentation on the basics of the different tools which will be in the PHOTOSHOP software. The session started at sharp 10 30AM in the Mini auditorium of GVPCE(A) b the speaker G.RAJESH KUMAR who is a 3rd year student of EEE dept.

The introduction part was taken nearly thirty minutes and in this gap the PHOTOSHOP software has been succesfully installed in the laptops of each and every participant. From 11:00AM they started designing different – different designs by using the software.

In the first stage they learnt different tools and later they were shown different ways and different techniques how to design the posters and flexys and how to design certificates and the speaker interacted with the students while teaching them how to design the things.

Btech students are motivated to participate in these type of technical workshops to improve their domain knowledge . This will be useful for their preparation towards on and off campus job recruitments. Hence more no of events will be organized by the students in future to build their career under IEEE vizag bay sub section.

The PHOTOSHOP workshop ended at 4:30pm with vote of thanks by Prof G.V.E. Satish kumar –IEEE SB Counsellor. The workshop was a huge success by the combined effort of IEEE SB executive members .
