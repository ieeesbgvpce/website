ROBOTICS_VIZAG: ONE DAY WORKSHOP ON FIREBIRD AND SPARK V ROBOTS

Date: 26 Jun 2019

Time: 09:00 AM to 05:00 PM

Event Category: Workshop

Event Summary

As proposed in the initial document for funding request, the event was successfully executed in line with the terms and conditions of IEEE Young Professionals. The event was well advertised and there was overwhelming response from IEEE student branch members in particular . The participants appreciated IEEE and the initiative for support of educational activity and are eagerly waiting for more events to come up. Many non IEEE members are keen in joining IEEE in view of availing the benefits that IEEE confers to students.

The local activity was organized as a 1 day event, the participants requested that the event be organized for 2 days for more information exchange and knowledge transfer. Due to hardware constraints the number that could be accomodated for the event was reduced to 35.
