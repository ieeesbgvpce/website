Event Summary

220 kV Substaion visit by 30 IEEE volunteers attended . Switch gear and various types of circuit breakers, transformers are seen during the visit.

We had a preview presentation of the substaion layout before the visit at the institute. It helped the students to answer the questions raised by the Engineers at the visit.
Overall the studetns were exposed to the substaion design and operation during the visit.
