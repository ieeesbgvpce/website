Event Summary

As a part of IEEE Day celebrations, technical events were conducted from 3rd to 5th October which included PowerPoint presentation, technical crosswords, circuit construction, reverse coding, and poster designing. The events were open to students of all branches and colleges. Certificates and mementos were distributed to the participants and winners respectively.

There was a satisfying response from all the participants in all the events. This is definitely a motivation for our student branch to conduct many more events in the upcoming days.
