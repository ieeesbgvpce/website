Steps to get membership online

Apart from many benefits offered by IEEE, the IEEE membership will enhance your professional and technical skills. IEEE student chapter is one of the student’s highest platform to be recognized world over for students in the field of Electrical, Electronics and Computer Engineering.

All those PG & UG students who are interested to become IEEE student member should follow the following steps.

Follow the procedure given below for creating your IEEE student membership online.

Visit www.ieee.org/membership/

After being directed to a new webpage, click on Join as a student.

A dialog box pops up asking to sign-in or create a new account.

Fill up your personal details in case you are creating a new account or sign-in if you already have an account.

Then you will be directed to membership and subscription Catalog page.

Select student member and add the item to your cart. The membership fare is US $27.00

Click on Proceed to Checkout option available in My cart.

Your purchase details and membership application details will be shown. Verify and proceed for payment after giving your shipment address.

Acknowledge the 2 boxes regarding the terms and conditions and checkout.

Now you have to choose the payment option.

If you are paying through credit card then fill up the details of credit card and continue.

For payment through check/DD, click on Print Order to Mail or Fax. It will show a proforma titled Print and Mail/Fax. Complete the proforma filling details of check/DD in the column check number.
