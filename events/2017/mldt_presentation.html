Event Summary

The event was held on 8th December 2017. It was organised by IEEE SB GVPCE (A) supported by IEEE Vizag bay subsection. It was a technical guest lecture given by A. Ramesh DE, APTRANSCO. The topic is an advanced technique of load dispatching performed nowadays. It has good scope for research in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. It is really useful to the students who want to pursue research or do project works and to those who would like to pursue their career in an industry. The topic is related to the faculty and to those who would like to pursue their career in an industry. The topic is related to the faculty and students of EEE (UG and PG). Students learn the new techniques of Load dispatching in power systems

The Technical lecture ended at 3 PM and has also witnessed the presence of Dr. Satish Kumar Gudey, IEEE student branch Counsellor, Dr. CVK Bhanu, Dean PG, DR. K. Parvatisam and Dr. V. Bapi Raju Senior Professors from Electrical Engineering Department.
