Two Day Workshop on Basics of MATLAB

Date: 17 - 19 September 2018

Time: 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM

Event Category: Workshop

Speaker:
Dr. G.V.E.Satish Kumar, Professor EEE Dept., IEEE SB GVPCE Branch Counsellor.

Event Summary

The event was held on 17th and 18th September 2018 at Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering(A) Madhurwada. It was organized by IEEE SB GVPCE(A) supported by IEEE vizag bay sub section .The event has begun at 10:20 AM on 17th September 2018.The gathering was addressed by Dr. G.V.E.Satish Kumar (Student Branch counsellor). It was followed by a presentation on the “BASICS OF MATLAB” by Dr. B Srinivas, Professor, Chemical Engg. dept., for the better understanding of the concepts for the students .

Nearly 30 students of both IEEE and Non-IEEE members were participated on the workshop.

The first session was a small lecture on the BASICS of MATLAB and the different tools which will be used in the MATLAB were explained by the speaker.

After the first session many of the technical questions and different models were solved by the students .

Btech students are well motivated to participate in these type of technical workshops to improve their domain knowledge . Such type of workshops will increase their technical knowledge towards the MATLAB software and useful for the on and off campus job recruitments. Hence more no of events will be organized by the students in future to build their career under IEEE vizag bay sub section.

The MATLAB workshop is ended at 3:30pm on 18th September with vote of thanks by Prof G.V.E. Satish kumar .

The feedback from the students is in a positive way so that they show more interest to participate in suh events conducted by IEEE.
